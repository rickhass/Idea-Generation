Instructions

In this experiment, you will be prompted to generate ideas about specific prompts. You will do so with your computer keyboard. It is important that you are sitting in your normal typing position and that you type as you normally would as if you were taking notes or writing in a Word document. Take the opportunity to ready yourself now for typing. You will practice typing before any of the real tasks start. There will be 7 trials total, split into 3 blocks, with each trial lasting 3 minutes except the first trial, which is a practice trial and will last 30 seconds. The three blocks as follows:

  1. 1 practice trial
  2. 3 experiment trials
  3. 3 experiment trials
