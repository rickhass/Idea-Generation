Sometimes the prompts will be simple: for example you may be asked to generate ideas about different things you can do to do with a piece of fruit.

Other trials will be more complex: for example you may have to read a sentence explaining some fact about the world and then generate ideas about the consequences of the statement.

No matter what, it is important that you type in every idea that pops into you your head as soon as it does. Try to type as fast as possible. It is not necessary that your idea "make sense", but try and describe your idea exactly as it came to you.

When you are ready to begin, press "Begin Practice." You will then be given further instructions about a practice trial
