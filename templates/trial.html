Task Instructions

The purpose of each of these tasks is for you to generate as many ideas as you can in the time allotted, but also to be as creative as possible when generating each idea

Before you start the creative thinking part, you will practice using the keyboard and text entry system.

When you press "Start", the name of a specific category will appear on the screen.

As soon as you think of an example of that category, type it into the field and press ENTER.

It is important to keep thinking of responses and to type them in for the entire time

Please type them in one at a time as they come to you, and press enter after entering each one.

Pressing enter will clear the response field so that you can type in the additional ideas that come to you

When the 30 seconds are over, you will recieve instructions about the first real experimental trial.

Task Instructions

In the next set of tasks, the goal is to think of uses for objects. Please be as creative as you like.

When you press Start, the name of a that object will appear on the screen.

As before, as soon as you think of something, type it into the field and press ENTER.

Do this as many times as you can in 3 minutes.

After 3 minutes on one category, the prompt will change to a new category, and after the next 3 minutes a third category will appear. This phase will last 9 minutes.

Remember,it is important to try to keep thinking of responses and to type them in for the entire time for each prompt

Please type them in one at a time as they come to you, and press enter after entering each one.

Pressing enter will clear the response field so that you can type in the additional ideas that come to you

Press continue when you are ready. If you would like to take a short break you may do so now, but remember that your HIT will expire after 60 minutes

Task Instructions

In this block of trials, a statement will appear on the screen. The statement might be something like "imagine that humans walked with their hands."

For 3 minutes, try to think of any and all consequences that might result from the statement.Please be as creative as you like.

When you press Start, the statement will appear on the screen for you to read.

As before, as soon as you think of something, type it into the field and press ENTER.

Do this as many times as you can in 3 minutes.

After 3 minutes on a prompt, the prompt will change to a new statement about consequences, and then it will change again to a third. This phase will last 9 minutes.

Remember,it is important to try to keep thinking of responses and to type them in for the entire time for each prompt

Please type them in one at a time as they come to you, and press enter after entering each one.

Pressing enter will clear the response field so that you can type in the additional ideas that come to you

Press continue when you are ready. If you would like to take a short break you may do so now, but remember your HIT will expire after 60 minutes.
